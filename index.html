Preparation

🡐 🡒
01 SKIN CHECK

Check your skin for sensitivity, irritation, or allergies beforehand. Healthy skin heals best.

02 REST WELL

Get a good night’s sleep the day before to help your body stay calm and pain-tolerant.

03 EAT BEFOREHAND

Don’t come to your session on an empty stomach. It helps to keep your energy and blood sugar stable.

04 NO ALCOHOL

Avoid alcohol 24 hours before the session. It thins your blood and increases bleeding.

05 HYDRATE

Drink plenty of water in the days leading up to your appointment to keep your skin supple.

06 COMFY CLOTHES

Wear loose, comfy clothes that allow access to the area being tattooed.

07 MOISTURIZE SKIN

Moisturizing a few days before helps the ink set better. Don’t apply lotion right before the session though.

08 PLAN THE DAY

Block off your day. Don’t rush or schedule stressful things afterwards. It helps your body relax.

09 SHAVE THE AREA

If advised, gently shave the area the day before. If unsure, let the artist do it for you to avoid cuts.

After Care

🡐 🡒
01 APPLYING & REMOVING WRAP

Keep the initial wrap on for 3–5 days. Remove it gently under warm water in the shower. Avoid wearing it longer – it can cause bacteria buildup. After removing the wrap, clean and reapply tattoo cream. If the tattoo is still very moist at night, a new wrap is okay. Otherwise, let it breathe freely.

02 WASHING THE TATTOO

After removing the wrap, wash the area with lukewarm water and optionally pH-neutral, disinfecting soap using only your hands. Avoid sponges or cloths. Gently pat dry with a clean, lint-free towel. Keep the tattoo clean and dry at all times.p>

03 APPLYING CREAM

Use tattoo cream (e.g. coconut oil or Hustle Butter) regularly. Don’t overdo it – too much cream can cause color loss or pimples, while too little can cause a thick crust that cracks and leaves scars or fades the ink.

04 AVOID DIRT & SWEAT

Keep the tattoo away from dirt and sweat to avoid infections. No sports or heavy physical activity for the first few days. Light activity after 1 week, intense workouts only after 2–3 weeks.

05 DO NOT SCRATCH

Itching is normal during healing, but never scratch! Scratching can damage the tattoo permanently.

06 NO SUN OR SOLARIUM

Avoid direct sunlight or tanning beds – they cause fading and increase the risk of sunburn on sensitive skin. Once healed, always apply sunscreen to protect your tattoo.

07 NO BATHING OR LONG SHOWERS

Avoid baths, long showers, swimming pools, lakes, or the sea during healing. Soaking can lead to color loss or infections.

08 AVOID FUZZY & TIGHT CLOTHES

Tight clothing irritates the tattoo, and fabric lint can cause infections. Stick to loose black cotton clothes, which absorb fluids well and won’t get ruined by ink stains.

09 TATTOO STUCK TO CLOTHING?

If your tattoo sticks to clothing, soak the area with warm water and remove the fabric slowly. Then clean and reapply cream.

10 HEALTHY NUTRITION = BETTER HEALING

Get enough Vitamin C to support healing. Avoid alcohol, as it destroys essential vitamins and slows recovery.
